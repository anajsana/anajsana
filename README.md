### 🧠 About Me

I'm an **open source community leader and data scientist** from Spain, with **5+ years of experience at The Linux Foundation** supporting global, multi-stakeholder developer ecosystems.

- 🤖 **[Agentic AI Foundation](https://aaif.io/) Ambassador**: helping developers and OSPO teams understand agentic AI projects and open standards **MCP**, **Goose**, and **AGENTS.md**
- 🔭 Currently active in: **TODO Group's Agentic AI to Empower OSPOs Working Group**
- 🤝 Co-chair of the **PyTorch Academic Outreach Working Group**
- 📊 Previously: **software development analytics at Bitergia**
- 🎓 MSc in Data Science (Rey Juan Carlos University): thesis on **measuring the health of open source developer ecosystems**
- 🎤 Speaker at: **GitHub Universe, the United Nations OSPOs for Good Symposium (now Uited Nations OSS Week), KubeCon + CloudNativeCon, FOSS Backstage, OSPO Summit China, Open Compliance Summit Japan, DevRelCon Tokyo & London, OpenInfra Days, and Open Source Summit events across Asia, Europe, and North America**
